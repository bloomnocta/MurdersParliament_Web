Elvira Martell

The Singer

A dazzling Harlem performer with a sharp mind and sharper instincts. Elvira climbed from poverty to the spotlight of New York’s nightlife, but fame came with chains attached. Beneath the feathers, glamour, and jazz melodies hides a woman determined to reclaim her voice, her dignity, and her freedom.
