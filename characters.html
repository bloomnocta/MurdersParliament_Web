Elvira Martell

The Singer

A dazzling Harlem performer with a sharp mind and sharper instincts. Elvira climbed from poverty to the spotlight of New York’s nightlife, but fame came with chains attached. Beneath the feathers, glamour, and jazz melodies hides a woman determined to reclaim her voice, her dignity, and her freedom.

Zina Volpe

The Scientist

A brilliant Italian-American scientist with a sharp tongue and an even sharper memory. After Basil stole credit for her discoveries, Zina dedicated herself to proving her genius to the world. Meticulous, calculating, and resentful, she never forgets an injustice.

Barkley Malone

The Detective

A street-smart detective with a nose for lies and trouble. Barkley Malone prowls the smoky alleys and jazz clubs of New York searching for the truth behind Basil Corvino’s murder. Beneath the trench coat and cynical attitude lies a loyal investigator who refuses to let corruption win.

Big Bear Romanov

The Ex-Enforcer

A towering ex-enforcer drowning in alcohol, rage, and old betrayals. Once powerful, now forgotten, Romanov survives in cheap motels while waiting for revenge against the man who ruined him. Dangerous, unpredictable, and desperate, he is a man with nothing left to lose.

Margaret Flynn

The Housekeeper

The strict but dependable housekeeper of the Corvino mansion. Margaret knows every locked door, whispered conversation, and hidden secret inside the house. Calm under pressure and fiercely observant, she has spent years keeping order while chaos brewed around her.

Thomas Doyle

The Butler

The mansion’s hardworking groundskeeper and handyman, known for his quiet strength and unwavering routine. Tommy keeps to himself, but years spent around Basil Corvino taught him that every luxurious estate hides something rotten beneath the surface.

Evelyn Parker

The Maid

A young maid with a warm smile and a quiet curiosity about the powerful people she serves. Evelyn often goes unnoticed by the mansion’s guests, allowing her to overhear conversations others would kill to keep hidden.

Samuel Rosen

The Houseman

A nervous but intelligent servant who handles paperwork, deliveries, and errands for the Corvino household. Sam avoids conflict whenever possible, though his sharp memory and attention to detail may make him more important to the investigation than anyone expects.

Josephine Caruso

The Maid

A bold and witty servant who refuses to be intimidated by the wealthy elite. Josie has seen enough corruption inside the Corvino mansion to lose faith in high society, but her fearless attitude may place her directly in danger.

Vincent Moretti

The Houseboy

A charming Italian-American servant who moves quietly through the mansion’s halls while listening more than he speaks. Loyal on the surface but always looking for opportunities, Vinnie understands that survival in New York often depends on knowing the right secrets.
